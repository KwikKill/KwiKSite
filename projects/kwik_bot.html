Kwik Bot

While I was watching IT videos on youtube in my free time, I got the idea to make a bot discord. First without any real purpose, then as a base for future projects. Kwik Bot was born (I swear I'm not megalomaniac).

This bot was quickly my biggest personal and self-taught project. I first developed it by trial and error with a lot of tutorials, then I got comfortable and moved on to the documentation of the Discord api. Once the base was done, I put this bot on a vps and I added features as the years went by. Some funny like the "B" answer when someone writes "AH" but others useful like a command to get the schedule of my school. My biggest improvement has been my League of Legends stats analysis commands detailed in a separate article.
